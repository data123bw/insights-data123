Featured publication

Botswana's crime data —
analysed, visualised, explained.

Our first intelligence publication transforms the official BPS Annual Crime Statistics Report into a fully interactive experience — four pages of charts, maps, and analytical commentary you can explore, filter, and cite.

📉 Crime declined for a second consecutive year — and 2024 sits below the pre-pandemic 2019 baseline.
🗺️ One third of all crime occurs in just two districts — Mogoditshane and Gaborone, mapped district by district.
⚠️ Murder is the warning signal — the only major offence rising every year since 2020, examined in depth.

About this hub

Why we publish
openly.

Most data consultancies keep their findings behind client confidentiality agreements. We believe that some of the most important intelligence about Botswana — crime patterns, labour markets, tourism trends, education outcomes — should be accessible to every organisation that needs it.

The Insights Hub is Data 123's contribution to evidence-based decision-making in Botswana. Every publication is produced to analytical standards, sourced from primary or official data, and reviewed before release.

Primary and official data sources
All reports cite primary data — government statistics, official surveys, and verified operational data.
Live interactive dashboards
Every annual report has a companion dashboard on insights.data123.online for interactive exploration.
Annual publication schedule
Reports are produced annually and updated when new official data becomes available.
No paywall. No registration required.
All published reports and dashboards are freely accessible. Subscribe only if you want new release notifications.
